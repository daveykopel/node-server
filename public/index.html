About

Charrua Birra y Empanadas is the creation of Carlos Uruguay. Founded in 2014 they are located on 150 Calle Cruz. Delicious empanadas and a wide variety of beer only begin to explain what makes this place so special. An area locals favorite, Birra y Empanadas has become a secret treasure spot in old san juan daily life. Many come for the delicious empanadas, but most come for its unique and magical setting. Open Monday to Saturday from 2pm to Midnight. Charruas Birra y Empanadas invites you to come and experience the magic.
